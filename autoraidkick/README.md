# Auto Raid Kick
Automatically scans your raid every 10 seconds and kicks anyone whose player name or guild name matches your blacklist.

Use `/blacklist` to open the manager window. From there you can add guilds or players separately, review the current blacklist, and remove entries. Guild entries are shown in green and player entries are shown in blue.
